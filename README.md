## Project 1 - Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard

**Tools:** Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, FPDF  
**Dataset:** Sample Superstore (10,000 rows)

### What I Built
- Cleaned and prepared 10,000 rows of raw retail sales data
- Built 11 professional charts including trend analysis, 
  profit breakdown and regional performance
- Created a master dashboard image showing all key metrics
- Built an automated PDF report generator that works on any new data

### Key Business Findings
- Discounts above 40% generate negative profit across all categories
- Technology has the highest profit margins - best category to focus on
- Q4 is peak season - revenue spikes 40% above average
- Capping discounts at 20% could recover $50,000+ in annual profit

### Skills Demonstrated
Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Data Cleaning, 
PDF Automation, Business Intelligence

## Project 2 - Customer Churn Prediction (Machine Learning)

**Tools:** Python, Scikit-learn, SQL, Pandas, FPDF  
**Dataset:** IBM Telco Customer Churn (7,043 customers)

### What I Built
- Analyzed 7,043 telecom customers to identify churn patterns
- Built machine learning model (Random Forest) predicting churn 
  with 80%+ accuracy and AUC score of 0.84
- Scored every single customer with HIGH, MEDIUM or LOW risk label
- Delivered an Excel file of high risk customers ready for 
  the retention team to contact immediately
- Created SQL queries for database integration in production
- Generated automated 8-page PDF report for client leadership

### Key Business Findings
- Month-to-month customers churn at 42% vs only 2.8% for two-year contracts
- Customers are most vulnerable in their first 12 months
- Electronic check payment users churn significantly more than auto-pay users
- Targeting high risk customers could save $200,000+ in annual revenue
- Retaining just 30% of high risk customers recovers $60,000+ annually

### Skills Demonstrated
Python, Machine Learning, Scikit-learn, SQL, SQLite, 
Data Visualization, PDF Automation, Business Intelligence
